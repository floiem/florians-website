Many Features

Sounds 🔉

Background music and sounds for every wrong or right answer.

Language
Change 🌐

Players can change the language between english and german.

Points 💯

Players are getting points for every right answer.

Highscore
Saver 💾

Points are saved to the local storage of the browser.

Price 🏅

When players are reaching 5/5 points they are getting a special price.

Startscreen 📺

The game is explained on a special start screen that only appears one time.

Individual Pixelgraphics 🖼️

The game shows an pixel graphic for every question.

Progress bar 🚧

The interface shows how far away from the highscore players are.

Highscore effect 🎉

Confetti appears when a player breaks a highscore.

Username saver 👤

You can save your highscore together with your name.

Test your knowledge

My main goal was to create the quiz for people to test their knowledge. You can collect points when answering questions right and get a price if you have every answer right after finishing the quiz.
