The process

Step 1

Sketches

After I recorded the interaction with the platform "Anchor" I began with creating a moodboard and some sketches to get more inspiration for my future work. In this example you can see how I tried to work with different diagram types.

Step 2

Miro Board

To bring everything in order, my goal was to create a clean Miro board where you can see what exactly happened at which specific time. This way, the work afterwards wasn't as much as it would have been without the Miro board.

Step 3

Mid Fidality Graphic

With the sketches (low fidelity graphics), the mood- and miroboard, I started with the mid fidelity variant of my interaction map. I wanted to make the design clean with a focus on readability while zooming in. Because of that, I have used vertical lines in the background at every time point to give readers the ability to zoom in and out without having to loose the focus.

Step 4

High Fidality Graphic

In the last step I created the high fidality graphic of my infographic. I put in images and made a few changes on the design (like you can see at the diagram or time steps.
