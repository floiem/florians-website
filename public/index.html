More to my person

It's not only design and technology, it's design WITH technology what I'm amazed about. When it comes to projects that are defining our future, I'm willing to participate in them and do the best I can to bring them to life.

In 2013 with the age of 12 I started looking up Blender, Cinema4D and AfterEffects Tutorials on Youtube and quickly went along many other Adobe software. I quickly realised that I love creating things for the digital world, so I started to sell my ability as a freelance video editor and my skills as an intro and brand designer. I have worked hard and got to know many people from all over the world. When Figma and other prototyping tools came out, I met another love of mine: user interface and user experience design. While studying at the University of Applied Design in Schwäbisch Gmünd (Germany) I learned how to write code in different programming languages and I count this to a very loved skill of mine too. My goal is to found and work on something that's a life-changer for humanity.
