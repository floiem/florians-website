Core Competencies

Designer and developers are often completely different people. But I try to combine both strengths.

Design

Increasing credentials for UX Design in modern technologies require professional techniques for the whole design process – from thinking to the final prototype.

Development

Implementing a product fast, safe and reliable is something that has to be done by someone who has both knowledge and practical experience in development.

Connections

Designers don't speak the language of developers and vice versa. A interpreter, such like I am, is needed to communicate between both teams in a company.
